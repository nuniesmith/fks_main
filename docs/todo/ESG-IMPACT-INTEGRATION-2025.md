### Key Challenges

1. **Greenwashing**: 70% cite concerns about misleading claims
   - **Solution**: Rigorous due diligence, transparent data

2. **Political Backlash**: Anti-ESG movements, policy reversals
   - **Solution**: Focus on long-term value, evidence-based approach

3. **Data Quality**: Inconsistent ratings, measurement gaps
   - **Solution**: Use multiple sources, qualitative assessment

4. **Performance Scrutiny**: Perceptions of underperformance
   - **Solution**: Long-term focus, comparable returns (12.5% vs 9.2%)

5. **Regulatory Uncertainty**: Policy shifts, disclosure changes
   - **Solution**: Diversify across regions, stay informed
