## ‚ö†Ô∏è Risk Considerations

### Potential Risks
1. **Volatility Increase**: Adding crypto may increase portfolio volatility
   - **Mitigation**: Limit to 10%, use futures for hedging
2. **Correlation Shifts**: Correlations may change in downturns
   - **Mitigation**: Stress-test via backtesting tools
3. **Futures Leverage**: Risk of liquidation with high leverage
   - **Mitigation**: Limit to 2-5x, use stop-losses
4. **Regulatory Changes**: Crypto regulations evolving
   - **Mitigation**: Stay informed, use regulated platforms

### Risk Management Rules
- **Position Limits**: 1-2% risk per trade (futures)
- **Stop-Losses**: 1-2% max loss per futures position
- **Rebalancing**: Quarterly or 5% drift
- **Diversification**: No single asset >10% (except BTC/ETH)
- **Leverage**: Max 5x on futures, avoid on spot
