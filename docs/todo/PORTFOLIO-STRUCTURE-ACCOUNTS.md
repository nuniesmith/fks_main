## ðŸ¦ Account Categories

### 1. Active Trading Accounts (Daily/Short-term)

**Purpose**: Active trading, day trading, swing trading  
**Time Horizon**: Days to weeks  
**Risk**: Higher risk, higher potential returns  
**Allocation Strategy**: Tactical, strategy-based

#### 1.1 Prop Firm Accounts
- **Type**: Proprietary trading firm accounts
- **Capital**: Varies by prop firm
- **Trading Style**: Day trading, scalping, swing trading
- **Instruments**: Futures, forex, crypto, stocks
- **Risk Management**: Strict drawdown rules (e.g., max 5%)
- **Focus**: High-frequency, short-term signals

**Characteristics**:
- Strict risk management rules
- Drawdown limits enforced
- Performance-based scaling
- May have trading restrictions

**Allocation Strategy**:
- Focus on liquid instruments
- Quick entry/exit capabilities
- Risk-first approach (1-2% per trade)
- No long-term holdings

---

#### 1.2 Personal Trading Accounts
- **Type**: Personal brokerage accounts for active trading
- **Capital**: Personal trading capital
- **Trading Style**: Day trading, swing trading, options
- **Instruments**: Stocks, options, futures, crypto
- **Risk Management**: Self-imposed rules
- **Focus**: Active signal generation and execution

**Characteristics**:
- Full control over trading decisions
- Flexible trading strategies
- Tax implications (short-term gains)
- Can hold positions longer than prop accounts

**Allocation Strategy**:
- Mix of strategies (scalp, swing, day trade)
- Signal-driven positions
- Active management
- Regular rebalancing based on signals

---

### 2. Long-Term Investment Accounts (Passive)

**Purpose**: Wealth building, retirement, financial independence  
**Time Horizon**: Years to decades  
**Risk**: Moderate to low risk, steady growth  
**Allocation Strategy**: Strategic, diversified, ESG-focused

#### 2.1 Retirement Accounts (RRSP/TFSA in Canada)
- **Type**: Tax-advantaged retirement accounts
- **Capital**: Long-term savings
- **Investment Style**: Buy and hold, DCA
- **Instruments**: ETFs, stocks, bonds, REITs
- **Risk Management**: Diversification, rebalancing
- **Focus**: Growth and income generation

**Characteristics**:
- Tax advantages (deferred or tax-free)
- Long-term focus
- Lower turnover
- Dividend/income focus

**Allocation Strategy**:
- 50% Stocks (diversified, ESG-focused)
- 15% ETFs (broad market, ESG)
- 15% Commodities (inflation hedge)
- 10% Crypto (BTC/ETH focus)
- 5% Bonds (stability)
- 5% Cash (opportunities)

---

#### 2.2 Taxable Investment Accounts
- **Type**: Standard brokerage accounts for long-term investing
- **Capital**: Additional investment capital
- **Investment Style**: Strategic allocation, rebalancing
- **Instruments**: Stocks, ETFs, bonds, alternatives
- **Risk Management**: Diversification, risk-adjusted returns
- **Focus**: Growth with ESG integration

**Characteristics**:
- Tax on capital gains and dividends
- More flexibility than retirement accounts
- Can include alternatives (REITs, commodities)
- Active rebalancing

**Allocation Strategy**:
- Follow 2025 optimization plan
- 20-30% ESG/Impact allocation
- Diversified across asset classes
- Quarterly rebalancing

## ðŸ“‹ Account-Specific Strategies

### Prop Firm Accounts Strategy

**Focus**: High-frequency, short-term trading

**Allocation**:
- **Futures**: 40-50% (micro contracts, quick exits)
- **Forex**: 20-30% (major pairs, scalping)
- **Crypto**: 20-30% (BTC/ETH perps, high volatility)
- **Cash**: 10-20% (opportunities, risk buffer)

**Risk Management**:
- Max 1-2% risk per trade
- Strict stop-losses
- Daily loss limits
- Drawdown monitoring

**Performance Metrics**:
- Win rate
- Risk-reward ratio
- Maximum drawdown
- Profit factor

---

### Personal Trading Accounts Strategy

**Focus**: Swing trading, options, active management

**Allocation**:
- **Stocks**: 40-50% (swing trades, signal-driven)
- **Options**: 20-30% (covered calls, spreads)
- **Crypto**: 15-20% (swing positions)
- **Futures**: 10-15% (trend following)
- **Cash**: 10-15% (opportunities)

**Risk Management**:
- 2-3% risk per trade
- Position sizing based on signals
- Portfolio heat management
- Correlation awareness

**Performance Metrics**:
- Sharpe ratio
- Sortino ratio
- Maximum drawdown
- Monthly returns

---
