## Tax Considerations (Canada)

### Capital Gains
- **Stocks/ETFs**: 50% of gains taxable
- **Crypto**: Treated as capital property (50% taxable)
- **Futures**: Mark-to-market (100% taxable)

### Tax-Advantaged Accounts
- **TFSA**: Use for crypto/stocks (no tax on gains)
- **RRSP**: Use for bonds/ETFs (tax-deferred)
